SCHEDULE I - PART A HireLock

The HireLock feature empowers companies to enhance joining rates and reduce no of back-outs after candidate accepts offer. HireLock offer can be designed to suit candidate profile. Choose from a one-way option for candidates to commit, or A two-way option enables both the company and candidate to commit. HireLock can be turned off or set for individual offers.

Candidates who accept HireLock offer commit to join when they accept offer and give consent that if they back out their information made available to future prospective employers.

SCHEDULE I - PART B Structured and Automated Communication

Companies can streamline communication strategy to enhance the candidate experience, and automated communications remove the burden of unstructured tasks. Companies can configure organization-specific communication for groups/ individuals and set emails to be fired on a fixed date/no. of days before or after a milestone.

Post offer engagement builds company, candidate rapport, and encourages result-oriented engagement.

PART C Advance Alerts

Companies can get advance alerts to warm them of potential drop-outs. With AI-assisted candidate communication analysis, email alerts are sent to warn about the candidates who are at risk of not joining. Alert conditions fully customizable based on business needs.
